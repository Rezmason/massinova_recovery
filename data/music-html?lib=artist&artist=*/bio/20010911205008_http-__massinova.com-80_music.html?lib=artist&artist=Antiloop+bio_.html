Antiloop features the talent of David Westerlund and Robin Soderman. Friends since childhood, being neighbors on Lindingo (an island in Stockholm), the pair began their musical trip together by vibing on a whole range of musical influences.

Classically trained, David is the musical brain of Antiloop and is clearly the genial type, keeping things in order although he's not always aware of it: "I'm incredibly absent minded and, I guess, bohemian", says David. "I sleep really long hours and I'm never on time for anything other than the studio. I prefer to work at night, so a lot of time I

end up trying to get some sleep with the mixer table as a pillow".

Robin is Antiloop's producer and is, as David says, "a fabulous sound technician who makes the perfect mix". As well as continuing to build their name as both the act, Antiloop, and as a production unit in itself, the partners spend a log of time as DJ's in Stockholm, at one point hosting a leading night in the capital, "The Rebels Room", also the name of their studio.
