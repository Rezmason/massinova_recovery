Simon Berry has had a long and involved career in the electronica music industry. Having first followed his passion at the age of 18, Berry moved to London where he could immerse himself in the music that he loved. Like most of the late-'90s producers, Berry found his love for electronica after hearing the techno tracks from the late-'80s of Chicago and Detroit. Hitting the clubs, such as Troll and Chemistry, he became enamored by the proto-trance classic "Acperience". He then set off on hisjourney to create his own sound.

His first release on his Platipus label, "Deeper Than Deep" caught

the attention of Carl Cox who agreed to a remix of the track in 1995. Berry quickly climbed the charts. Collaborating with future Terra Ferma producer Claudio Guisanni as Union Jack, resulting in "Two Full Moons and a Trout". The following year saw Berry's full-length album on Platipus, "Wildlife on One". It is known asa cornerstone album and among the first to feature the proto-trance sound growing in Germany and the U.K.
