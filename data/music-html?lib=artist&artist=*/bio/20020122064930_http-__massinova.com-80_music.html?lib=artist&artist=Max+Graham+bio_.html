Having traveled since he was born in 1971, Max Graham, living in London, Spain, Los Angeles and New York before settling in Ottawa, speaks through his music as an attentive and insightful observer. He started spinning in 1986, when He first touched a pair of turntables at the tender age of fifteen. Over the fourteen years of his career, Max has dabbled in many kinds of dance music. Originally a scratch DJ, He met house music in the early nineties, at the beginning of what would become a renaissance period for dance music across the globe.

In these landmark years between 1992 and 1995 the

music world would witness the birth of progressive house, and with it such artists as BT and Underworld, and such DJs as Sasha and Paul Oakenfold to push the envelope to even futher reaches into trance and beyond. Max was one of the first DJs in Canada to pick up on this distinctive sound, and he went to work interpreting it for himself.
