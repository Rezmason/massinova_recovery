ATC   [ Music ]

Comprised of talents covering the corners of the world, ATC (A Touch of Class) turned a friendship into a global experience. Their single breakout "Around The World (La La La La La)" has sent ATC to the top of international charts as well as making them award winners and multple-award nominations. ATC debuted on Republic/Universal Records, arriving in the US on February 6, 2001 utilizing their dynamic, upbeat dance grooves, enticing ballads and sexy vocals that give their music a common appeal among fans around the world.

Based in Hamburg, Germany, ATC is compromised of early

twenty-somethings Joe (New Zealand), Sarah (Australia), Tracey (England), and Livio (Northern Italy) who met as cast members in the German production of "Cats". While proving their dance skills in "Cats", it was the foursome's vocal crafts honed since childhood that propelled them into a best-selling act. As Joe recalls, "We really wanted to show the strenght of our voices and not just our dancing. We all had the same pop dreams. We're all just as strong as singers as we are dancers. In our live performances we have a massive dance show, but it's the songs that show all our talents."
